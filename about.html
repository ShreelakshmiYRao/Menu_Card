ABOUT US

Our Mission

At Frosty Bliss, our mission is to bring joy to our community through delicious, handcrafted ice cream made from the finest ingredients. We believe in creating memorable experiences, one scoop at a time.

Our Story

Sweet Scoops was founded in 2010 by two childhood friends, Emma and Raj, who shared a passion for ice cream and a dream of opening their own parlor. Starting with a small shop in the heart of the city, we have grown into a beloved local favorite, known for our unique flavors and friendly atmosphere.
